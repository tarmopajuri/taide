FILLIO PRIVACY POLICY
Effective from: 06.03.2026

1. GENERAL INFORMATION

Fillio (fillio.eu) is a construction material sharing platform managed by Tardek.
Data controller: Tardek
Contact: tarmo@tardek.com
Website: https://fillio.eu

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

2.1. When creating an account:
- Name (username)
- Email address
- Password (stored in encrypted form)

2.2. When adding a listing:
- Contact person's name
- Phone number (optional)
- Email address (optional)
- Address / location (coordinates)
- Uploaded images

2.3. Automatically collected data:
- Session cookies (for login)
- IP address (server logs)

3. PURPOSES OF DATA USE

Your data is used for the following purposes:
- Managing and authenticating user accounts
- Publishing and managing listings
- Enabling communication between users
- Ensuring and improving platform functionality
- Ensuring security

4. DATA SHARING

We do not share or sell your personal data to third parties, except:
- Contact information published in listings is visible to all platform users
- Legal obligations (upon request by law enforcement authorities)

5. DATA RETENTION

- User account data is retained until the account is deleted
- Listing data is retained until the listing is deleted
- Server logs are retained for up to 90 days

6. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Restrict data processing
- Object to data processing
- Data portability

To exercise your rights, contact: tarmo@tardek.com

7. SECURITY

We use various measures to protect your data:
- Passwords are encrypted (bcrypt hash)
- HTTPS encryption for all web traffic
- Regular security auditing

8. CHANGES

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy. Users will be notified by email of significant changes.
