FILLIO PLATFORM RULES
Effective from: 06.03.2026

1. GENERAL RULES

1.1. Fillio is a platform that enables users to share leftover construction materials.
1.2. Using the platform requires agreement with these rules.
1.3. Fillio reserves the right to modify the rules, notifying users of any changes.

2. USER ACCOUNT

2.1. A valid email address and name are required to create an account.
2.2. Each user may have only one account.
2.3. Users are responsible for the security of their account (keeping their password safe).
2.4. Account information must be truthful and up to date.
2.5. In case of account misuse, please notify us immediately: tarmo@tardek.com

3. ADDING LISTINGS

3.1. You must be logged in to add a listing.
3.2. A listing must include:
    - Material type
    - Title (accurate and descriptive)
    - Contact information (at least a name)
    - Location (correct address and map marker)

3.3. A listing must be:
    - Truthful and accurate
    - In Estonian or English (or understandable)
    - Related to construction materials

3.4. Permitted materials:
    - Stone, sand, soil, gravel, concrete
    - Other construction and fill materials
    - Used construction materials in good condition

4. PROHIBITED CONTENT

4.1. It is prohibited to post:
    - Hazardous or environmentally dangerous materials (asbestos, hazardous waste)
    - Stolen materials
    - Materials whose sale is prohibited by law
    - Offensive, discriminatory, or illegal content
    - Spam or duplicate listings
    - Materials unrelated to construction
    - General commercial advertising

5. CONTACT INFORMATION IN LISTINGS

5.1. Contact information must be truthful.
5.2. Contact information is visible to all platform users.
5.3. Users are responsible for publishing their own contact information.

6. MANAGING LISTINGS

6.1. Users can delete their own listings.
6.2. Expired listings should be deleted promptly.
6.3. Fillio administrators have the right to remove listings that violate the rules.

7. LIABILITY

7.1. Fillio is an intermediary platform and is not responsible for:
    - The quality of materials
    - Agreements between users
    - Damages arising from the use of materials
7.2. Users communicate and make agreements among themselves.
7.3. Fillio does not participate in transactions and does not process payments.

8. RULE VIOLATIONS

8.1. In case of rule violations, Fillio may:
    - Remove listings that violate the rules
    - Warn the user
    - Restrict or close the user account
8.2. Repeated violations may result in permanent account closure.

9. CONTACT

For questions and suggestions:
Email: tarmo@tardek.com
Website: https://fillio.eu
