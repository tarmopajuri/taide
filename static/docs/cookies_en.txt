FILLIO COOKIE POLICY
Effective from: 06.03.2026

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device. They help the website remember your preferences and provide a better user experience.

2. WHAT COOKIES WE USE

2.1. Essential cookies (session cookies)
- Purpose: Managing login sessions
- Duration: Until browser is closed or session expires
- Type: First-party cookie
- Required: Yes, necessary for platform functionality

2.2. Third-party cookies

a) OpenStreetMap / Leaflet map service:
- Purpose: Displaying interactive maps
- Provider: OpenStreetMap Foundation
- More info: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

b) Google Fonts:
- Purpose: Loading web fonts
- Provider: Google LLC
- More info: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. MANAGING COOKIES

You can manage cookies in your browser settings:
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- Firefox: Settings > Privacy and Security
- Safari: Preferences > Privacy
- Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent the platform from functioning properly (e.g., login will not work).

4. ANALYTICS

We currently do not use separate analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics). If this changes in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.
